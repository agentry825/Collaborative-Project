The Power of Computing in Communication

Breaking down barriers or just letting your boss contact you 24/7?

Before computers were a household staple, long-distance communication was often costly and time-consuming. Letters took a long time to reach their recepient, and phone calls, especially internationally were prohibitively expensive. However, developments in computing have made international communication not just easy, but taken for granted.

A Timeline of Communication Developments

  1. 1971 - Email
  2. 1983 - The Internet
  3. 1988 - Instant messaging
  4. 1996 - SMS
  5. 2004 - The start of social media
  6. 2020 - Online working
